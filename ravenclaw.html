Ravenclaw

Characteristics

Fondator: Rowena Ravenclaw

House colors: Blue and bronze

Animal: Eagle

Main feature: Wisdom

Leader: Filius Flitwick

Spirit: Grey Lady

Common room: Ravenclaw Tower

Details

Ravenclaw is one of the four houses of Hogwarts, the School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, founded by Rowena Hawkeye. The members of this house are characterized by their intelligence, creativity, and wisdom. The colors of the house are blue and bronze, and its symbol is the falcon. The ghost of the house is the Gray Lady, who in life was Rowena Hawkeye's daughter. Many experts in magic came from this house, including Filius Flitwick and Luna Lovegood. Hawk-eye corresponds to the air element.


The sitting room is located in the Hawkeye tower on the west side of Hogwarts. The living room is round and filled with blue drapes and armchairs, has a vaulted ceiling painted with stars, and has a statue of Rowena wearing a diadem. The tower has a spectacular view of the surrounding mountains. A riddle must be solved to gain entry.


Harry Potter

The Harry Potter volumes form a very popular series around the world; they belong to the fantasy genre and were written by the British author J. K. Rowling. The books deal with a wizarding world, with the protagonist being a young wizard named Harry Potter, along with his friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.
