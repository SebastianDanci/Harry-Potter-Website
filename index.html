    Details

    The Harry Potter Universe

    The world of magic that Harry lives in is completely separate, but also very close to our world. While the fantasy universe of Narnia is an alternative universe to our own, and the Lord of the Rings universe represents a mythical past, the magical world of Harry Potter exists at the same time as our own, with magical elements analogous to our own.

    A world of magic

    The ability to produce magic is acquired through family rather than learning it, however those who have this ability must attend a school, like Hogwarts, to learn to use and master magic.

    Abilities

    Wizards whose parents have magical abilities and both of their parents come from parents with magical abilities are "purebloods" (eg: Ronald Weasley), and those who have only one parent with magical abilities or have only one parent with "blood- pure", and the other has "mud-blood" they have "semi-pure blood" (ex: Harry Potter).

    Quidditch

    Players use flying brooms to move through the air. The aim of the game is to catch the "Golden Snitch", a small and fast ball, as quickly as possible.
