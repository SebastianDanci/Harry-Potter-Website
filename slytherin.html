Common room: Slytherin Dungeon

Detalii

The Vipers are one of the four houses at Hogwarts, School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, and are home to students who exhibit traits such as cunning and ambition. Its emblem is the snake and its colors are green and silver. Founded by Salazar Viperin, the house is composed mostly of pureblood students, due to its founder's distrust of the Locked Witches. The house has a negative reputation, with many claiming that it is the source of the Darkest Wizards in Britain, especially Tom Dorlent Cruplud, also called the Skull Lord, as well as many of the Death Eaters.


Vipers tend to be ambitious, shrewd, cunning, and achievement-oriented. They also developed senses of self-preservation. This means that Vipers tend to hesitate before acting, so that they weigh all the possible outcomes of a decision (and how those outcomes would have a personal impact), unlike The Scouts, whose natural courage might lead to action immediate and instinctive. According to Albus Dumbledore, the qualities that Salazar chose for his students included intelligence, ingenuity, determination, and "a certain drive to break the rules." Dumbledore noticed that all these qualities were possessed by Harry Potter, who arrived at The Scouts.


Salazar's legacy is a locket, which was turned into a Horcrux by Voldemort, and then destroyed by Ron Weasley with the Sword of Godric Gryffindor. The bedrooms and living room of the Vipers is under the Black Lake, it is dungeon style, furnished with green lamps and carved armchairs. Joben Magic claims that Vipers choose their students by blood purity. One can enter the living room of the Vipers by saying a password.

Harry Potter

The Harry Potter volumes form a very popular series around the world; they belong to the fantasy genre and were written by the British author J. K. Rowling. The books deal with a wizarding world, with the protagonist being a young wizard named Harry Potter, along with his friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.
