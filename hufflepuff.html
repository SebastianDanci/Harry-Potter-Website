Hufflepuff

Characteristics

Founder: Helga Hufflepuff

House colors: Yellow and Black

Animal: Badger

Main feature: Loyalty

Leader: Pomona Sprout

Spirit: Fat Friar

Common room: Hufflepuff Basement

Detalii

Hufflepuff had in its house children who were very orderly, who did nothing but good and whom the world considered "milk daddy". The bedrooms and living room are hidden behind a stack of barrels in a corner to the right of the corridor leading to the kitchen. To get in, you have to play some sticks near the door to the rhythm of Helga Astropuff's name. Unlike other houses, the Astropuffs' living room has a bouncer that makes entry illegal if the pace is wrong. The living room is decorated with yellow curtains, small tunnels lead to the bedrooms with perfectly round doors.


The Hufflepuff Cup - an ancient artifact, once belonged to one of the founders of Hogwarts - Helga Hufflepuff. In the mid-20th century the cup was stolen by Lord Voldemort from its last owner Hepzibah Smith, and turned into a Horcrux. For this transformation Voldemort killed Mrs. Smith.

Harry Potter

The Harry Potter volumes form a very popular series around the world; they belong to the fantasy genre and were written by the British author J. K. Rowling. The books deal with a wizarding world, with the protagonist being a young wizard named Harry Potter, along with his friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.
