Harry Potter and the Cursed Child

Harry Potter and the Cursed Child is a two-part stage play based on a new original story by J.K. Rowling, John Tiffany and Jack Thorne. It is the eighth story in the Harry Potter series and the first one officially performed on stage. The world premiere of the play took place on July 30, 2016, at a London theater in the West End. This volume contains the script of the rehearsal with the audience and invites us to take part in a new episode in the saga of Harry Potter, his friends and his family. The film's script was released to the public on July 31, 2016, and is the eighth official story in the Harry Potter series. The story of the play takes place in 2017 and 2020, 19 years after the events of Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows, and follows the events of Harry Potter and his son.


It's always been hard for Harry Potter to be himself, and now that he's working to exhaustion at the Ministry of Magic, has a wife and three school-going children, it's not much easier.


As Harry wrestles with a past that refuses to stay where it belongs, his youngest son, Albus, also struggles with the weight of a family legacy he never wanted. When the past and the present merge menacingly, both father and son discover an uncomfortable truth: sometimes darkness comes from where you least expect it.


Jack Thorne writes for theatre, film, television and radio. He is the author of the songs Hope and Let the Right One In, among many others. His screenplays include War Book and The Scouting Book for Boys, and for television he wrote the BAFTA-winning Don't Take My Baby, The Fades and This Is England. He is currently working on an adaptation of Philip Pullman's Dark Matters trilogy for the BBC.


John Tiffany has won numerous awards for his work as a director, both in West End theaters and on Broadway. Among the plays he directed are The Glass Menagerie, Macbeth, Bacchante, Let the Right One In and Black Watch. He is Associate Director of the Royal Court Theater and was Associate Director of the National Theater of Scotland from 2005 to 2012.


Harry Potter

The Harry Potter volumes form a very popular series around the world; they belong to the fantasy genre and were written by the British author J. K. Rowling. The books deal with a wizarding world, with the protagonist being a young wizard named Harry Potter, along with his friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.
